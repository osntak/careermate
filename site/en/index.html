Web and mobile AI apps can't reach local tools on your computer, so they aren't supported.

Your data never leaves your computer

The dashboard server binds only to 127.0.0.1 (this computer), so it can't be reached from outside.
The only thing the MCP server sends out is an optional update check (you can turn it off) — your career data is never transmitted. The analysis and writing are done by the AI you chose yourself.

  • Storage ~/.careermate/careermate.sqlite
  • Export cover letters and more to ~/.careermate/exports
  • Delete & manage right in the dashboard Settings
